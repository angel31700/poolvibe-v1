The problem

Pool care shouldn't feel like guesswork

Every pool owner has been there — staring at a test strip, unsure what to add, hoping the pool store gets it right.

🧪

Test strips don't tell you what to do next

You read the colors, but then what? Most owners are left guessing how much of what to add — and the label math assumes you know your gallons.

📐

Most owners don't know their pool gallons

Without an accurate volume estimate, every chemical calculation is off. You can be wrong by 20% before you even open a bottle.

🛒

Chemical buying is a guessing game

Pool store apps and calculators don't know your pool. You end up buying too much, too little, or the wrong product entirely.

📋

Maintenance companies can cut corners

Weekly service doesn't always mean thorough service. Underdosing is common — and you'd never know from the invoice.

⏱️

Chemical order matters — most get it wrong

Add shock before lowering pH and you waste half of it. Sequence matters, and no one tells you this at the pool store.

💸

Wrong decisions add up fast

Wrong chemicals, wrong quantities, and bad advice compound into real money — and a pool that never quite looks right.
