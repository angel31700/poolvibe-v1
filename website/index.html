The problem

Pool care shouldn't feel
like guesswork

Every pool owner has been there — staring at a test strip, unsure what to add, hoping the pool store gets it right. It doesn't have to be this way.

🧪

Test strips don't tell you what to do next

You read the colors, but then what? Most owners are left guessing how much of what to add — and the label math assumes you know your gallons.

📐

Most owners don't know their pool gallons

Without an accurate volume estimate, every chemical calculation is built on a shaky foundation. Off by 20% on gallons means off by 20% on dose.

🛒

Chemical buying is a guessing game

Pool store apps and calculators don't know your pool. You end up buying too much, too little, or the wrong product — then the problem comes back.

📋

Maintenance companies can cut corners

Weekly service doesn't always mean thorough service. Underdosing and rough assumptions are common — and you'd never know from the invoice.

⏱️

Chemical order matters — most get it wrong

Add shock before lowering pH and you waste half of it. Add alkalinity and pH adjusters at the same time and they fight each other. Sequence matters.

💸

Wrong decisions add up fast

Wrong chemicals, wrong quantities, and bad advice compound into real money — and a pool that never quite looks right.
