The problem

Most pool owners
are guessing —
and paying for it

  • 🧪

    Test strips give readings, not doses. They tell you pH is 7.9. They don't tell you how much acid to add for a 17,000 gallon pool.

  • 📐

    Most owners don't know their gallon count. Without it, every dosing calculation is a guess — or copied from the label.

  • 📋

    Maintenance reports can be generic. "Added 1 lb shock" says nothing about whether that was right for your pool size and condition.

  • 🛒

    Buying chemicals is confusing. Wrong product, wrong strength, wrong amount — and the problem comes right back.
